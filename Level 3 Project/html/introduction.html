Why is Sous Vide Better?

Easier and More Consistent

Cooking steak sous vide allows you to control both the temperature and the flavor with precision, resulting in a better steak.

Time-Saving

While the steak is cooking, you have time to make something else, watch a movie, or even design a website!

Tastier and Healthier

Sous vide cooked steak doesn't need as much added seasoning, and it won't lose the original weight or flavor.
