Too Little Perfect Too Much
Doneness A rare steak should be lightly charred on the outside, browned around the sides, and bright red in the middle. A medium rare steak should be warm through the middle and most of the center should be pink in color with a hint of red. A well-done steak will not have the faintest hint of pink in the middle and be browned through.
Sear The outside is barely browned, with plenty of gray still visible. A nice deep brown crust. The outside is overly burnt and charred.
Flavor The steak isn't salted enough and has no flavor. Just the right amount of seasoning to compliment the beef's flavor. There is too much salt or other seasoning, overpowering the beef.
Juciness The steak is dry. The steak is juicy. I don't think this is possible.
